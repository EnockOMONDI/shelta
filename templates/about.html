About Shelta Cost

A Kenyan Consultancy Serving Building and Civil Engineering Projects

Building Trust
Since 2014

Shelta Cost Solutions Ltd was founded in 2014 and incorporated on 16 January 2015. The firm was established to offer flexible, timely and dependable consultancy services in quantity surveying for building and civil engineering works, and has since expanded into contract administration, project management, construction management, feasibility studies, project monitoring and evaluation, and dispute resolution support.
