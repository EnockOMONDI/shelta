2014

Shelta Cost Solutions Is Founded

The firm begins operations with a focus on quantity surveying consultancy for private dwellings and building works.

2015

Company Is Incorporated

On 16 January 2015 the company is incorporated as a private limited liability company under the laws of Kenya.

2018

BORAQS Registration

The firm is licensed by the Board of Registration of Architects and Quantity Surveyors under registration number 258Q.

2021

AAK Firm Membership

Shelta Cost becomes a firm member of the Architectural Association of Kenya Quantity Surveyors Chapter under member number F00206.

Today

Service Expansion and Sector Growth

From private residences the portfolio has grown to apartments, factories, office complexes, retail outlets, bank fit-outs, schools, hotels, hostels and sports facilities, supported by expanded services in contract administration, project management, construction management, feasibility studies and dispute resolution.
