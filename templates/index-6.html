Common Inquiries

Questions Clients Ask Before Engaging Us

1. What services does Shelta Cost Solutions provide?

We provide quantity surveying, contract administration, project management, construction management, feasibility studies, project monitoring and evaluation, and dispute resolution and management.

2. Which sectors have you worked in?

Our experience covers luxury and middle-income apartments, bank and office fit-outs, commercial developments, industrial complexes, retail outlets, schools, social facilities, hotels, hostels and sports facilities.

3. Are you professionally registered?

Yes. The firm was licensed by BORAQS in 2018 under registration number 258Q and became a firm member of the AAK Quantity Surveyors Chapter in 2021 under member number F00206.
